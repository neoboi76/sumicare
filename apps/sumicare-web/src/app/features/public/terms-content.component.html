1. Reservation Types

New Lasema Spa Jjimjilbang offers two reservation types. A hard reservation is paid in full before it is confirmed and guarantees the selected time slot and a room for that slot. A soft reservation records a preferred time slot and an optional preferred therapist, does not require upfront payment, and does not reserve a room in advance. Soft reservations always yield to hard reservations when slots or rooms are allocated.

2. Guaranteed Slot and Late Arrival

For a hard reservation, your time slot and room are held for the booked window. If you arrive late, the appointment end time remains fixed and you receive only the remaining time within your booked window; the full amount still applies. A preferred therapist is never guaranteed for either reservation type and is subject to availability.

3. Cancellation

Soft reservations may be cancelled at any time at no cost since no room is held and no payment is taken. Hard reservations cancelled at least 24 hours before the scheduled time are eligible for a refund per the policy below. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled time may not be refundable.

4. Refunds by Payment Method

Eligible refunds are returned through the original payment method. Card and GCash payments are reversed to the same account and may take several business days to reflect, depending on the provider. Cash payments made at the front desk are refunded in cash at the spa. Service fees charged by the payment provider may be non-refundable.

5. Timeliness and Lateness

Please arrive at least 15 minutes before your scheduled time for preparation. If a reserved client is 10 to 20 minutes late, the forfeited slot may be offered to a waiting walk-in client. Sessions follow predetermined, fixed durations per service type.

6. Booking and Order Policies

A nickname and a valid email address are required for every booking. Clients are identified by nickname and email only; real names are not recorded in operational documents. Prices, inclusions, and durations follow the published service and package catalogue at the time of booking.

7. Client Responsibilities and Restrictions

Clients must disclose any relevant medical or skin conditions before a session and follow the practitioner's reasonable instructions. Services are provided for relaxation and wellness only and are not a substitute for medical care. Any inappropriate, unsafe, or abusive conduct will result in immediate termination of the session without refund. The spa is not responsible for personal belongings unless secured in a provided locker.

8. Data and Consent

By proceeding, you consent to the spa storing your booking details for the purpose of fulfilling and improving your reservation, including usage history used for service recommendations and voucher eligibility. A copy of these Terms and Conditions is emailed to you with your booking confirmation.
