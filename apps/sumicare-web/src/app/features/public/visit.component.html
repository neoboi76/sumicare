Before You Arrive

The small things make the day

What to bring · how to get here · house rules

What to Bring

Shampoo, conditioner, body wash, facial wash, and lotion in a small shower caddy. We provide the jjimjilbang uniform, locker, towel, and slippers. Bring cash — not all services accept cards.

How to Get Here

Sampaloc Street at the corner of Estrella, San Antonio Village. Street parking is available; ride-share drops you at the door. From EDSA, take the Estrella exit and head west for two blocks.

House Rules

Disclose any medical conditions before booking a massage or scrub. Children may stay in the jjimjilbang at the child rate. Any illicit remarks toward staff end the session with payment forfeited.
