The Beginning

Founded in 2005, Lasema began as a single-floor sauna serving the small Korean community in Makati. Over twenty years it has grown into a multi-storey jjimjilbang offering the full spectrum of Korean wellness practices: kiln-room therapy, hot-and-cold contrast bathing, scrub treatments, and a full massage menu drawn from Korean, Filipino, Thai, and Hawaiian traditions.

Today Lasema operates around the clock for guests staying up to twelve hours per visit, with kiln rooms named for the materials that line their walls — Amethyst, Himalayan Salt, Ochre, Pine Tree, and Ice. The Sleeping Cave and entertainment hall give you a quiet place to rest or watch a movie between sessions.

Our therapists are vetted, salaried, and trained to read the room. The decking system rotates therapists fairly so every team member gets equal opportunity and every guest gets a fresh, focused therapist.
