1. Booking and reservations

Two reservation types are supported on this site. A soft reservation communicates your intent to visit on a given date and time; we hold the slot only on a best-effort basis. A hard reservation is paid in full at the time of booking and reserves the slot for you. Late arrivals on hard reservations are accommodated within the remaining time of the slot; the full fee remains payable.

Soft reservations may be cancelled at any time without charge. Hard reservations cancelled at least 24 hours before the scheduled time will be refunded in full. Cancellations within 24 hours of the scheduled time are non-refundable except where the spa is unable to render the service.
